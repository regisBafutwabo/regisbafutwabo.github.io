Destino

This web app is designed to assist travelers all over the world in planning and documenting their trips. At present there are websites which allow users to either plan their trips or document their trips. Our goal is to provide a platform which supports both of these requirements. Through our web app the users can post photos of their travels, view other users’ travels, comment, like, and share them, get weather and currency information, and reserve hotels, tour packages, and flights. This system shall help the users by cutting short the time and effort spent while blogging and planning trips using multiple websites.
